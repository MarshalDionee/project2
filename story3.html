TONY STARK

Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a fictional character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). He is portrayed as a genius, billionaire, playboy, and philanthropist, played by Robert Downey Jr. Stark is known for his wit, intelligence, and resourcefulness, often delivering memorable and inspiring quotes throughout his MCU appearances. Some of his most iconic quotes include "I am Iron Man," "I love you 3000," and "The truth is... I am Iron Man"[1][3]. His character has undergone significant growth and development throughout the MCU, evolving from a weapons manufacturer to a superhero and leader of the Avengers[2].
